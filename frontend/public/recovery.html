Seed Phrase Recovery

An offline tool you run on your own computer to recover a partial BIP39 seed phrase.

What this is

A single self-contained HTML file (~514 KB). When you open it on your computer it runs entirely in your browser — no server, no internet, no installation. You give it the words you remember and your wallet's public address (0x…); it iterates BIP39 candidates locally and tells you the full phrase when it finds a match.

Your seed phrase NEVER leaves your computer. Not to me, not to any server, not over the network. The page even has a network-block policy header that prevents the browser from making any external requests.

Before you download

  1. Verify the URL in your browser bar. It should be the address I sent you. If it doesn't match exactly, close this tab.
  2. Have your wallet's public receive address ready (the 0x… string, 42 characters). Without it the tool cannot verify any candidate phrase.
  3. Have the words you remember written down on paper next to you, with their position numbers (1–12 or 1–24).
  4. Close other browser tabs and apps — the search will use your CPU heavily.

2. Disconnect from the internet

Turn off Wi-Fi. Unplug ethernet. Switch to airplane mode if on a laptop.

The tool doesn't need internet, and disconnecting protects you in case anything tries to phone home. The page itself blocks all network requests via Content-Security-Policy, but disconnecting is the belt-and-suspenders move.

3. Open the downloaded file

Find recover.html in your Downloads folder. Double-click it. It opens in your default browser at a file:// URL (no server involved).

You should see a banner at the top:

  • If it's RED — you're still online. Disconnect first, then refresh the page.
  • If it's GREEN — you're offline. Safe to continue.

4. Fill in the form

  • Phrase length: 12 or 24 (whichever your wallet uses).
  • Target ETH address: paste your wallet's public 0x… address.
  • Path template: leave the default m/44'/60'/0'/0/{i} unless your wallet is Ledger Live, in which case use m/44'/60'/{i}'/0/0.
  • Word grid: for each position, type either:
    • a single BIP39 word you remember — that position is fixed
    • ? or leave blank — any BIP39 word
    • a semicolon-separated pool, e.g. cat;dog;bird — restricted candidates

Click Start search. A progress bar shows how it's going.

5. When a match is found

  1. Write the phrase on paper IMMEDIATELY. Do not screenshot. Do not paste into any app. Do not message it to me.
  2. Close the browser tab.
  3. Delete the recover.html file.
  4. Empty your trash / recycle bin.
  5. Reconnect to the internet only after the file is gone.
  6. Immediately move all funds from this wallet to a brand-new wallet with a brand-new seed phrase. The recovered phrase has been on multiple devices and should be considered compromised.
